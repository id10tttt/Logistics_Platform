Inventory Barcode Scanning

This module adds support for barcode scanning to the Inventory Management & will also help in excelling delivery process.

Features
  • Scans and delivers products effortlessly.
  • Search existing products by barcode & internal reference of the product.
  • Use barcode to deliver products in incoming & outgoing deliveries.
  • Reduces operational time consumed in manual entry of products.
  • Reduces probability of human error while adding the product in delivery.
  • A warning message pops up each time when trying to scan product more than ordered quantity.
  • A warning message pops up each time when a wrong barcode is scanned.

Delivery View
